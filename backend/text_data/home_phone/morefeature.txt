Xfinity Voice features quick reference guide

Depending on your Xfinity Voice plan, you can access to up to 11 of the most popular calling features:

Three-way calling*
Anonymous call rejection*
Call forwarding*
Call forwarding selective*
Call return*
Call screening*
Call waiting
Caller ID
Caller ID blocking per call
Caller ID with call waiting
Repeat dialing*



*Not included with Xfinity Voice Unlimited Saver plan.

Note: Call Return, Repeat Dialing and Speed Dial are available in some areas (not all).

Features quick guide

You can turn your Voice features on or off at any time. Just press the star (*) button and the following numbers:

Feature	Turn on	Turn off	Available through the Voice settings on xfinity.com
Anonymous call rejection	*77	*87	Yes
Call forwarding	*72	*73	Yes
Call forwarding selective	*63	*83	Yes
Call return**	*69	*89	No
Call screening	*60	*80	Yes
Call trace	*57	 	No
Call waiting	 	*70	No
Caller ID blocking per call	*67	*82	No
Repeat dialing	*66	*86	N/A
Speed dial 8 (set up)	*74	 	No
Speed dial 30 (set up)	*75	 	No
Three-way calling	*96	*97	N/A


**For your safety, your Caller ID information is always available when you call public emergency hotlines, poison control lines, and 911 services, even if you have Caller ID blocking turned on.
